Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how EcoPlanta Consulting ("we", "us", and "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control our use of them.

By continuing to browse or use our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or other device) by a website’s server through your web browser. They help the website remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, font size) over a period to enhance your browsing experience.

Cookies can be "session cookies" which are deleted when you close your browser, or "persistent cookies" which remain on your device until they expire or are deleted.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use cookies for several purposes, including:

  1. Strictly Necessary Cookies: These help the website function properly, enabling core features like security, network management, and accessibility. They cannot be disabled as they are essential.
  2. Performance Cookies: Collect information on how visitors use our site, such as pages visited and any errors encountered. This data improves site performance and usability.
  3. Functionality Cookies: Allow the site to remember choices you make, like language or region, to provide enhanced features and personalized content.
  4. Targeting or Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements to you, based on your interests, and to measure the effectiveness of ad campaigns.
  5. Analytics Cookies: Help us monitor site traffic and usage to better understand trends and user behavior to improve the website experience.

Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. You have the option to block, delete, or accept cookies on your device. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience and some features on our site may not function correctly.

Consent to Use Cookies

When you first visit our website, a cookie consent dialog will appear allowing you to accept or decline cookies. Your choice will be stored, and we will respect your preferences for your subsequent visits.

You can also change your preferences at any time by adjusting your browser settings or through the consent dialog if available.
